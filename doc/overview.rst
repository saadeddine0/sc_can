Overview
========

The XMOS CAN component implements the CAN 2.0 specification. The data rates
which are achievable are:

+=============+==================+
| Bit rate    | MIPS required    |
+=============+==================+
| 500 kbps    | 31.25            |
+-------------+------------------+
| 1 Mbps      | 125              |
+-------------+------------------+
